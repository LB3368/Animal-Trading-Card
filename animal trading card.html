Elephants

Elephants are the world's largest land mammal.

  • Two species of Elephants: African and Asian
  • Average Life Span: 48 - 70 years
  • Average Weight: 6,000 - 13,000 lbs.
  • Average Meal: 149 - 169 kg of vegetation daily

Elephant, (family Elephantidae), are grayish to brown in colour, and their body hair is sparse and coarse.
